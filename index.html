Studio Appartment

is the most common type of apartment in big cities because it is compact while still allowing for all the necessary areas. It is one room with an open floor plan, which means that your bedroom, kitchen, and living space are all together in one area.

Micro Appartment

are one-room apartments, roughly less than 350 square feet. They will include a space for sitting, sleeping, a bathroom and a kitchenette.

Loft Appartment

generally has one large, open room with high ceilings. Other characteristics include large, high windows, exposed brick and support beams, as lofts are often found in commercial buildings that have been renovated.
